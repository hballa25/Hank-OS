# Trading Goals & Rules

*Built via /grill-me interview, 2026-07-06. The Trader reads this before every daily plan.*

## The mission
Learn day trading the safe way: mimic one popular, proven, fully rule-based strategy, prove it **virtually first** on paper, and only then trade real money. Research → paper → live, in that order, no skipping.

## Markets & phase
- **Instrument:** Micro E-mini futures first — MES/MNQ, 1 contract. Stocks come later, after the futures strategy is proven.
- **Current phase:** PAPER ONLY on TradingView paper trading. No real money is at risk in this phase.
- **Planned live account:** under $1,000 (fund only after the gate is passed).

## Schedule
- **Now (summer):** flexible, most weekday mornings — target window is the market open, roughly 9:30–11:30 AM ET.
- **School year:** deliberately undecided. Revisit after the paper phase with real data on what the strategy needs.

## Risk rules (apply to paper exactly as they will to live)
- **Max daily loss: $50.** Hit it → done for the day, platform closed, no exceptions.
- **Risk per trade: $10–25** (position sizing works backward from the stop).
- **Fully rule-based checklist:** if the checklist isn't met, there is no trade. No discretion, no "feel."
- Experience level: brand new — rules exist precisely because instinct doesn't yet.

## The go-live gate 🔒
Real money only after **one calendar month of paper trading that is BOTH:**
1. **Net profitable**, and
2. **Zero violations** — never broke the $50 daily loss limit, never took a trade outside the checklist.

The Trader audits both in the daily journal. A profitable month with violations does NOT pass. Restart the clock after any violation month.

## Strategy
- To be selected from research into popular mechanical strategies (ORB, VWAP pullback, etc.) — see [[Strategy Playbook]] once written.

## Decisions
- Micro futures over stocks first: no PDT rule, tiny per-point risk, fits small account (chosen in interview)
- $50/day loss limit (Hank's pick — higher than the recommended $20; balanced by the zero-violations gate clause)
- One-month gate **with the discipline clause** rather than pure P&L — luck-proofing the fast gate
- TradingView paper trading as the platform: best charts, built-in paper account, browser + phone
