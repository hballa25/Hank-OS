# Fitness Goals

*Built via /grill-me interview, 2026-07-06. The Chef aligns every weekly [[meal-plan]] to this file.*

## Goal
**Recomp — lose fat while gaining muscle.** Starting weight 280 lb, lifting 3–5x/week. At this starting point recomp runs on a real calorie deficit + very high protein: fat comes off while newer-lifter muscle still builds. Best of both, no bulk/cut cycles.

## Daily targets (v1 — adjust with weigh-in data)
- **Calories: ~2,400–2,600** (roughly a 750 kcal deficit off an estimated ~3,300 maintenance; expect ~1.5–2 lb/week down early)
- **Protein: ~200g/day** — the non-negotiable number; it's what protects and builds muscle in a deficit
- **Creatine: 5g daily**, any time, every day including rest days
- Carbs/fat: fill the remainder, carbs biased around training sessions

## Structure
- **2 meals + 1 shake**: big lunch (~80g protein), big dinner (~80g protein), shake (~40–50g with milk or double scoop)
- **Sunday batch prep**: one lunch recipe × repeated all week (Hank confirmed repetition is fine) + dinner components
- **No food restrictions.** Budget-normal.

## Tracking
- Weigh-in: daily if tolerable, same time each morning; The Chef reads the weekly average, not single days
- Adjustment rule: weekly average not dropping ~1.5 lb/week for 2+ weeks → drop 200 kcal (protein stays at 200g)

## Decisions
- Deficit-recomp over pure surplus/cut (right call at 280 + lifting consistently)
- 2 meals + 1 shake pattern (Hank's pick — fewer, bigger meals, easy school-day fit)
- One lunch recipe per week (max prep efficiency)
